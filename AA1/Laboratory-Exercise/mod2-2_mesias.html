Bungou Stray Dogs

Synopsis: This is an anime about gifted people with supernatural powers. Some of them used their powers to do good, solve mysteries, and carry out missions, while others used it to create chaos. Some of its characters are named before famous authors in Japan. The anime also gives us an idea of how different people view life, and how their views could affect their decisions and personalities. Overall, it is a great anime.

My Top 5 Favorite Characters

  1. Ranpo Edogawa
  2. Osamu Dazai
  3. Ryunosuke Akutagawa
  4. Atsushi Nakajima
